our services

We're pleased to offer the following services
  • Wellness Examinations
  • New Puppy/ Kitten examinations
  • Pet Vaccinations
  • In-house state of the art Laboratory for Pre-anesthetic blood exam, senior blood exam, Phenobarbital test, Cortisol test, Diabetes exam, Thyroid test, Brucellosis Test, Pregnancy test, Heartworm Test, Parvo Test, Giardia Test, Cushing's Disease(ACTH stimulation) test, Fecal examination and Urine analysis
  • X-Rays and Glaucoma testing machine
  • Dental exams, cleaning, polishing and extractions
  • Elective surgeries- cat and dog spays and neuters
  • Soft tissue surgeries -for lacerations, tumor removals, foreign body removal from gastro-intestinal tract, hernias, cherry eye, entropion, enucleation, feline de-claw, tail dockings, bladder stone, urethrotomy, pyometra
  • Hospitalization ** subject to availability **
  • Heart worm prevention and treatment
  • External and internal parasite treatment
  • Veterinary Diets
